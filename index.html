About NSS SCE

NSS SCE is the student society wing of NSS belonging to the School of Computer Engineering of KIIT-DU. With a total of 7 sub-projects namely Aarogya, Aashayein, Go Green, Lipi, Roshni, Saarthi & Vikaas and more than 200 active volunteers, NSS SCE aims to provide our community proper help, guidance and assurance in various fields such as social and communal responsibility,equity,cleanliness,environmental concerns,etc. Along with this NSS SCE also aspires to provide quality education and guidance regarding various issues, concerns and necessities to underprivileged students in many schools.

About NSS SCE AAGHAAZ 2019

The Flagship event of NSS SCE , AAGHAZ; successor of its previous flagship event Happiness Concert aims to attain a plethora of grandeur through the message which NSS provides day in and day out along with a celebration through different competitive platforms being provided with many social, technical and cultural events.
The foundation stone of Happiness concert and now NSS Aaghaz is laid to create a platform for the students to express their most exceptional talents and potential to excel in different fields such as different forms of art, oratory, technology, mental ability, sports and many more.
